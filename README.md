## Problem Statement

Many people ignore early dental symptoms due to lack of awareness and access to quick guidance. People often cannot identify whether their dental symptoms are minor or severe, leading to delayed treatment and poor oral health decisions. An accessible AI-powered system is needed to provide preliminary guidance, help users understand possible dental conditions, and assist them in deciding when to seek professional dental care.

## Solution

Smart Dental Care is an AI-powered platform that helps users identify possible dental conditions, understand the seriousness of their symptoms, and make informed decisions about seeking professional care. The platform includes an AI chatbot that provides preliminary guidance, answers common dental health questions, and helps users determine when they should visit a dental clinic.

## Future Scope

- Advanced AI Chatbot: Enhance the chatbot to provide more human-like conversations, personalized dental consultations, and better guidance based on user symptoms and history.
- Improved Image Analysis: Upgrade the AI disease detection system to more accurately identify dental conditions and provide severity levels (mild, moderate, or severe) along with appropriate recommendations.
- Smart Appointment System: Improve the appointment module to automatically suggest suitable dentists or specialists based on the detected condition and user location.
- Personalized Dental Health Tracking: Allow users to maintain their dental history, reports, and treatment progress for long-term oral health monitoring.
- Voice-Based Assistance: Integrate voice interaction so users can communicate with the AI assistant more naturally.
- Teleconsultation Support: Enable direct online consultations with dental professionals through the platform.
- Personalized dental health tracking
